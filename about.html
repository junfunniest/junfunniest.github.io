Our story

JapBite was born from a deep love for authentic Japanese flavors and a dream to bring a cozy yet modern dining experience to Singapore. Inspired by izakayas and traditional street food stalls, our journey began in 2024 with one goal — to share Japan’s comforting dishes in a fresh, friendly setting.

Our mission

At JapBite, we believe in more than just food — we believe in experience. That’s why every dish is made with care, every ingredient is selected for quality, and every guest is treated like a friend.

Meet the Team / Our Chef

Chef Riku brings 15 years of experience from Tokyo’s finest kitchens to JapBite. Passionate about fresh ingredients and authentic flavors, he crafts every dish with care — especially his signature teriyaki sauce. Our friendly team shares his dedication to quality and great service, making sure every visit feels special.
